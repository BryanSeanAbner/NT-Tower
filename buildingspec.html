Building Specification

Trinity Tower is a new iconic office building located in the heart of CBD Jakarta that offers prestige and elegance for rising and established enterprises alike. This new office tower for lease offers synergetic design that reflects both combination of excellent Japanese architecture and the elegance of Indonesian craftsmanship.

Developed by PT Windas Development, a joint venture company among Mitsubishi Estate Asia, The Gesit Companies, Shimizu Corporation, and Santini Group, Trinity Tower is a combination of cultures reflected on its simplicity of design, state of the art technology and various retail facilities to satisfy the need of its discerning tenants.
